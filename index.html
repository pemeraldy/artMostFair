CELEBRATING NIGERIS'S INDEPENDENCE

The ArtMostFair is an experiential event designed to harness and showcase the narratives of Nigerian creatives

N igeria's creative industry is the fastest growing in the world. Accounting for at least 2.3% of the nation’s GDP, creativity is an export item that presented a positive image of Nigeria to the world. The ArtMostFair is an experiential event designed to harness and showcase the narratives of Nigerian creatives through notable artistic expressions. The focus of the event is to highlight the nation’s unique cultural diversity and tell a story of Nigeria’s vibrance and beauty. The event is necessary to retell the tales of Nigeria’s diversity and drive a narrative about inclusiveness, unity and national cohesion. All this while attracting tourist attention to Nigeria’s art and culture. With a grand event scheduled to celebrate Nigeria’s 60th Independence Anniversary, the ArtMostFair is a story about Nigeria.

OBJECTIVES OF THE EVENT

Indentity talented Nigerian creatives spanning across 36 states

Promote Nigerian talent and culture to a global reach

Create art to celebrate Nigerian culture
